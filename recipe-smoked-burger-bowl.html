Smoked Burger Bowl

Estimated Time to Completion: ~1 hour

Equipment

  • Smoker
  • Cast iron skillet
  • Spatula
  • Mixing bowl

Ingredients

  • 1–2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika

Base

  • Cooked white rice

Toppings

  • Lettuce
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Red onion (optional)

Burger Sauce

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped pickles

How To

  1. Preheat smoker: Set smoker to 225°F.
  2. Season beef: Add ground beef to a bowl and mix in salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika.
  3. Smoke beef: Place seasoned ground beef in a cast iron skillet. Put skillet in smoker and cook about 1 hour, breaking up and stirring occasionally until fully cooked.
  4. Make burger sauce: Mix mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, and chopped pickles.
  5. Build bowl: Layer rice, smoked ground beef, lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and cheese.
  6. Finish: Drizzle burger sauce over the top and serve.
