THM (S) Taco Beef Bowls

Estimated Time to Completion: 20–25 minutes

Equipment

  • Stovetop
  • Large skillet
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • 1.5–2 lbs ground beef
  • ½ cup water

Seasoning

  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Sides (S Compliant)

  • Shredded lettuce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Avocado slices
  • Salsa (no added sugar)

How To

  1. Cook beef: Heat skillet over medium heat and cook ground beef until browned.
  2. Add seasoning: Stir in all seasoning and water.
  3. Simmer: Let cook 5–7 minutes until thickened.
  4. Assemble: Build bowls with lettuce base, taco beef, cheese, sour cream, tomatoes, avocado, and salsa.
  5. Serve: Eat immediately.
