Smoked Rib Nachos

Estimated Time to Completion: 20 minutes

Equipment

  • Baking sheet
  • Aluminum foil
  • Knife and cutting board

Ingredients

  • Leftover Smoked Baby Back Ribs (meat removed from bone)
  • 1 bag thick-cut tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack or Sharp Cheddar
  • 1/2 cup pickled jalapeños
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • Sour cream

How To

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Remove cold rib meat from bones and chop into bite-sized chunks.
  3. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  4. Layer: Chips, half the cheese, half the rib meat. Repeat for a second layer.
  5. Bake for 8–10 minutes until cheese is melted.
  6. Top with jalapeños, BBQ sauce drizzle, and sour cream.
  7. Serve immediately.
