Coffee & Cocoa Smoked Ribeye

Estimated Time to Completion: ~1.5–2 hours

Equipment

  • Smoker
  • Cast iron skillet or grill (for sear)
  • Meat thermometer
  • Tongs

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak(s)
  • 2 tbsp finely ground dark roast coffee
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ancho chili powder
  • Salt
  • Coarse black pepper

How To

  1. Prep steak: Pat ribeye dry.
  2. Season: Mix coffee, cocoa, paprika, brown sugar, and chili powder. Apply salt and pepper, then coat steak evenly with the rub.
  3. Rest: Let steak sit at room temperature for about 40 minutes.
  4. Preheat smoker: Set to 225°F.
  5. Smoke: Place steak on smoker and cook until internal temperature reaches about 115°F.
  6. Sear: Transfer to a hot cast iron skillet or grill. Sear about 60 seconds per side until a crust forms.
  7. Rest: Let steak rest 5–10 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve.
