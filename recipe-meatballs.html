Meatballs

Estimated Time to Completion: 40–50 minutes

Equipment

  • Oven
  • 9×13 baking dish
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Spoon or hands for mixing

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Sauce

  • 1 cup grape jelly
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

How To

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix meatball ingredients: In a large bowl combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, garlic, parmesan, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form meatballs: Roll mixture into golf ball sized meatballs.
  4. Place in dish: Arrange meatballs evenly in a 9×13 baking dish.
  5. Bake: Cook 20–25 minutes until mostly cooked through.
  6. Add sauce: Mix grape jelly and BBQ sauce together and pour over the meatballs.
  7. Return to oven: Bake another 10–15 minutes until meatballs reach 165°F internal temperature.
  8. Stir gently so sauce coats everything before serving.
