Sloppy Joes

Estimated Time to Completion: 25–30 minutes

Equipment

  • Stovetop
  • Large skillet or pan
  • Spatula or spoon

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

How To

  1. Brown the beef: Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef until browned.
  2. Add onion and garlic: Stir in diced onion and minced garlic. Cook 3–4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add sauce ingredients: Stir in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, mustard, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer: Reduce heat and let mixture simmer 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally until thick.
  5. Serve: Spoon onto buns and serve hot.
