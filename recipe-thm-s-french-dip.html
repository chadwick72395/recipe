THM (S) French Dip Roast & Au Jus

Estimated Time to Completion: 6–8 hours

Equipment

  • Crockpot or Dutch oven
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Tongs

Ingredients

  • 2–3 lb chuck roast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp butter

Seasoning

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder

Sides (S Compliant)

  • Steamed green beans with butter
  • Side salad (lettuce, cheese, cucumber, ranch or olive oil)

How To

  1. Prep roast: Season all sides of the chuck roast.
  2. Sear (optional): Heat oil in pan and sear roast 2–3 minutes per side.
  3. Slow cook: Place roast in crockpot with beef broth and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Cook: Low 6–8 hours until fork tender.
  5. Shred: Pull apart or slice thin.
  6. Au jus: Use the cooking liquid as dipping sauce, add butter and stir.
  7. Sides: Cook green beans and assemble salad.
  8. Serve: Plate beef with au jus on the side for dipping.
