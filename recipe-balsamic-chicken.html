Balsamic Chicken (Caprese Style)

Estimated Time to Completion: 25–30 minutes

Equipment

  • Cast iron skillet
  • Stovetop
  • Oven
  • Tongs or spatula

Ingredients

  • 1.5–2 lbs thin sliced chicken breast (or boneless thighs)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

How To

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Heat cast iron skillet with olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear chicken about 3–4 minutes per side.
  5. In a small bowl mix balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, honey, and Italian seasoning.
  6. Pour the balsamic sauce around the chicken in the skillet.
  7. Place slices of fresh mozzarella on top of the chicken.
  8. Transfer skillet to oven and cook 10–15 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F.
  9. Optional: Add cherry tomatoes and basil before serving.
