Smoked Wings

Estimated Time to Completion: 2–2.5 hours

Equipment

  • Smoker (pellet smoker recommended)
  • Meat thermometer
  • Tongs
  • Mixing bowl (optional for seasoning)

Ingredients

  • 2–3 lbs chicken wings (whole or flats/drums)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika

Optional Glaze

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce

How To

  1. Preheat smoker to 225°F.
  2. Pat wings dry.
  3. Toss with olive oil and seasonings.
  4. Arrange on smoker grates with space between pieces.
  5. Smoke ~2 hours, flipping halfway through.
  6. Cook until internal temperature reaches at least 175°F.
  7. If glazing, melt butter and hot sauce and brush on during the last 10–15 minutes.
  8. Serve immediately.
