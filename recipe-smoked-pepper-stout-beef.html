Smoked Pepper Stout Beef

Estimated Time to Completion: 5–6 hours

Equipment

  • Smoker
  • Large aluminum pan
  • Aluminum foil
  • Meat thermometer
  • Forks (for shredding)

Ingredients

  • 3–4 lb Beef Chuck Roast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp salt and pepper mix
  • 2 large bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 jalapeños (seeded and sliced)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 12 oz Stout beer
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce

How To

  1. Preheat smoker to 275°F.
  2. Season chuck roast and smoke directly on grates for 3 hours.
  3. Place sliced peppers, onions, garlic, stout, and Worcestershire in the aluminum pan.
  4. Transfer beef to the pan and cover tightly with foil.
  5. Cook for 2–3 more hours until internal temp is 205°F.
  6. Rest for 15 minutes, then shred the beef into the juices and vegetables.
  7. Serve on rolls or over potatoes.
