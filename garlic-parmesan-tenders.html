Garlic Parmesan Tenders

Estimated Time to Completion: 30–35 minutes

Equipment

  • Oven
  • 9×13 baking dish or sheet pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Knife (to remove tendon from tenderloins)

Ingredients

  • 1.5–2 lbs chicken tenderloins
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional, for pan)

How To

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Prepare chicken: Remove the white tendon (devein) from each tenderloin if present.
  3. In a mixing bowl combine mayonnaise, minced garlic, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and parmesan.
  4. Lightly oil a 9×13 baking dish or sheet pan.
  5. Add chicken tenderloins to the bowl and coat them fully in the garlic parmesan mixture.
  6. Lay the coated tenderloins evenly in the baking dish.
  7. Bake for 20–25 minutes until the chicken reaches 165°F internal temperature.
  8. Optional: Broil for 2–3 minutes at the end to lightly brown the top.

Simple, creamy, and very garlic-forward.
