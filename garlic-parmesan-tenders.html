Garlic Parmesan Tenders

Estimated Time to Completion: 30–35 minutes

Equipment

  • Oven
  • 9×13 baking dish or sheet pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Knife (to remove tendon from tenderloins)

Ingredients

  • 1.5–2 lbs chicken tenderloins
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional, for pan)

How To

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Prepare chicken: Remove the white tendon from each tenderloin.
  3. In a bowl mix mayonnaise, minced garlic, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and parmesan.
  4. Lightly oil a 9×13 baking dish or sheet pan.
  5. Coat chicken: Toss the tenderloins in the garlic parmesan mixture until fully coated.
  6. Arrange chicken evenly in the baking dish.
  7. Bake 20–25 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F internal temperature.
  8. Optional: Broil 2–3 minutes at the end to lightly brown the top.
