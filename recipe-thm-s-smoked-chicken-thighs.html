THM (S) Smoked Chicken Thighs

Estimated Time to Completion: 1.5–2 hours

Equipment

  • Smoker
  • Meat thermometer
  • Tongs
  • Small bowl
  • Skillet (for sides)

Ingredients

  • 6–8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter (optional)

Seasoning

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1–2 tsp monk fruit sweetener

Sides (S Compliant)

  • 1 lb green beans
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lettuce or mixed greens
  • Shredded cheese
  • Cucumbers
  • Ranch or olive oil dressing

How To

  1. Preheat smoker: Set smoker to 250°F.
  2. Prep chicken: Pat chicken thighs dry and lightly coat with olive oil.
  3. Season: Mix seasoning and apply evenly on all sides.
  4. Smoke: Place thighs skin side up and cook 60–75 minutes.
  5. Increase heat: Raise smoker to 375°F and cook another 20–30 minutes until internal temp reaches 175–185°F.
  6. Finish: (Optional) Brush melted butter on thighs during last 10 minutes.
  7. Green beans: Melt butter in skillet, add garlic and green beans, cook 8–10 minutes until tender.
  8. Salad: Assemble greens, cheese, cucumbers, and dressing.
  9. Serve: Plate chicken with sides and serve hot.
