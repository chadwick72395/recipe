THM (E) Smoked Lemon Herb Chicken & Rice Soup

Estimated Time to Completion: 6–8 hours

Equipment

  • Smoker
  • Dutch oven or cast iron pot with lid
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Spoon or ladle

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4–5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Optional: small splash of apple cider vinegar at the end

How To

  1. Preheat smoker: Set smoker to 225°F.
  2. Build soup base: Add chicken broth, chicken breast, onion, garlic, bay leaf, thyme, oregano, salt, and black pepper to the Dutch oven.
  3. Smoke: Place the pot in the smoker and cook for 5–6 hours with the lid on.
  4. Shred chicken: Remove the chicken breast, shred it, and return it to the pot.
  5. Add rice: Stir in the uncooked white rice and continue cooking 1–2 more hours until the rice is tender.
  6. Finish: Stir in lemon juice, lemon zest, and optional splash of apple cider vinegar.
  7. Serve: Remove bay leaf and ladle hot soup into bowls.
