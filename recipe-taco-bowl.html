Frito Taco Bowl (Crockpot)

Estimated Time to Completion: 6–8 hours (low) or 3–4 hours (high)

Equipment

  • Crockpot / slow cooker
  • Skillet (for browning beef)
  • Spoon

Ingredients

  • 1–2 lbs ground beef (lean preferred)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 can tomato sauce (or 2–3 tbsp tomato paste + water)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Cooked rice (for serving)
  • Fritos (for serving)

How To

  1. Brown beef: Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain excess grease.
  2. Add to crockpot: Transfer beef to crockpot.
  3. Add ingredients: Add onion, garlic, black beans, corn, tomato sauce, and all seasonings. Stir to combine.
  4. Cook: Cover and cook on low for 6–8 hours or high for 3–4 hours.
  5. Stir: Mix everything well before serving.
  6. Build bowl: Add rice to a bowl, top with beef mixture, then add Fritos on top.
  7. Serve.
