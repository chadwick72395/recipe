THM (E) Smoked Honey Soy White Fish & Rice

Estimated Time to Completion: 45–60 minutes

Equipment

  • Smoker
  • Meat thermometer (optional)
  • Tongs or spatula
  • Small bowl
  • Pot or rice cooker

Ingredients

  • 4 white fish fillets (cod, tilapia, or mahi mahi)
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups water

Sauce

  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger (optional)

How To

  1. Preheat smoker: Set to 225–250°F.
  2. Cook rice: Prepare white rice according to instructions.
  3. Mix sauce: Combine soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger.
  4. Prep fish: Pat fish dry and lightly coat with sauce.
  5. Smoke: Place fish on smoker and cook 20–30 minutes until flaky.
  6. Finish: Brush additional sauce on fish during last 5–10 minutes.
  7. Serve: Plate fish over rice and spoon extra sauce on top.
