THM (E) Ninja Foodi Chicken Fried Rice

Estimated Time to Completion: 25–30 minutes

Equipment

  • Ninja Foodi
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Ingredients

  • 1–1.5 lbs chicken breast, diced
  • 2 cups cooked white rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, etc.)
  • 2–3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

How To

  1. Preheat Foodi: Set to sauté or high heat mode.
  2. Cook chicken: Add diced chicken and cook until fully done.
  3. Add garlic: Stir in minced garlic and cook 1 minute.
  4. Add vegetables: Mix in vegetables and cook 2–3 minutes.
  5. Scramble eggs: Push everything to one side, crack eggs in, scramble, then mix together.
  6. Add rice: Stir in cooked rice and break it up.
  7. Season: Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  8. Finish: Cook another 3–5 minutes until heated through.
  9. Serve: Serve hot.
